KKMMPTC Kallettumkara Thrissur

K. Karunakaran Memorial Model Polytechnic College (KKMMPTC), established in 1993 under Institute of Human Resources Development, has grown from a basic technical institute into a modern innovation hub. Set on a 12.375-acre campus with AICTE-approved programs, it introduced specialized courses like Diploma in Biomedical Engineering and strengthened industry placements. In recent years, the college embraced digital learning, partnered with Kerala State Start-up Mission, and launched initiatives like Career Catalyst and Industry on Campus. Events such as Tarang Mela 2k26, themed “Agentic AI,” highlight its focus on advanced fields like RPA and VLSI, making it a leading center for technical education.
